What We Do

Discover how LOMHS is "Transforming Communities with Health, Equality, Sustainability, and Opportunity."

LOMHS provides comprehensive services to empower communities and promote sustainable development. From ensuring access to quality healthcare and reproductive health education to advancing gender equality, eco-friendly agriculture, renewable energy solutions, and environmental conservation, we strive to create a healthier, more equitable, and resilient future for all.

Healthcare Access and Reproductive Health

What We Do: Provide essential healthcare services, rehabilitation resources, and reproductive health education to underserved communities.

How We Do It:

  • Mobile clinics and healthcare outreach programs.
  • Workshops on HIV/AIDS, drug abuse, and family planning.

Impact: Reduced maternal mortality and empowered individuals.

Gender Equality and Advocacy

What We Do: Empower women, girls, and the disabled through education and awareness programs.

How We Do It:

  • Scholarships and vocational training.
  • Dialogue on gender-based violence.

Impact: Inclusive communities with equal opportunities.

Sustainable Agriculture and Green Energy

What We Do: Promote eco-friendly farming practices and renewable energy solutions.

How We Do It:

  • Climate-smart agricultural training.
  • Distributing solar and biogas systems.

Impact: Enhanced food security and reduced environmental degradation.

Environmental Conservation Programs

What We Do: Protect ecosystems and combat deforestation.

How We Do It:

  • Tree-planting and reforestation campaigns.
  • Community education on waste management.

Impact: Sustained biodiversity and climate resilience.
