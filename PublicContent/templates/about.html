About Us

Empowering Lives, Sustaining Futures

Welcome to Lean On Me Health and Sustainable Solutions (LOMHS)

A dedicated non-governmental organization committed to fostering healthier, prosperous, and environmentally conscious societies.

Our Mission

To empower communities through equitable access to quality healthcare, gender equality, and reproductive health services, while fostering sustainable agricultural practices, environmental conservation, and the adoption of green energy solutions for a resilient and prosperous future.

Our Vision

A world where communities thrive with equitable healthcare, gender equality, and sustainable practices for a prosperous and environmentally resilient future.

Our Core Values

Equity and Inclusion

Commitment to ensuring access to resources and opportunities for all, regardless of gender, race, or socioeconomic background.

Sustainability

Advocacy for practices and policies that protect the environment and promote the responsible use of natural resources.

Empowerment

Supporting individuals and communities to become self-reliant and active participants in their development journey.

Innovation

Leveraging cutting-edge solutions in healthcare, agriculture, and green energy to address community challenges.

Collaboration

Building strong partnerships with stakeholders to achieve shared goals and maximize impact.

Integrity and Transparency

Maintaining high standards of integrity, openness, and responsibility in all initiatives and operations.

Resilience

Encouraging adaptive and forward-thinking approaches to overcome social, economic, and environmental challenges.

Compassion

We approach our work with understanding and respect for every individual and community we serve.

Our Services

Healthcare Access

Rehabilitation and reproductive health education to improve well-being.

Gender Equality

Empowering women, girls, and people with disabilities through advocacy and education.

Sustainable Agriculture & Green Energy

Eco-friendly farming techniques, renewable energy adoption, and training for environmental conservation.

Environmental Conservation

Reforestation and climate change awareness programs.
