Lean On Me Health and Sustainable Solutions (LOMHS) is a dedicated non-governmental organization working to create healthier communities and sustainable environments. Through innovative healthcare services, environmental projects, and community empowerment, we are building a better tomorrow for all.

Our Key Services

Healthcare Access and Reproductive Health

Providing essential healthcare services, rehabilitation, and reproductive health education to empower individuals and improve community well-being.

Gender Equality and Advocacy

Promoting women, girls, and people with disability empowerment, gender rights, and equality through education, capacity-building, and advocacy initiatives.

Sustainable Agriculture and Green Energy Solutions

Supporting communities with eco-friendly farming techniques, renewable energy adoption, and training to boost productivity, health, and environmental conservation.

Environmental Conservation Programs

Implementing projects that protect natural ecosystems, promote reforestation, and create awareness about climate change and sustainability.

Partnerships and Collaborations

LOMHS collaborates with local governments, international NGOs, and private institutions to amplify its impact and reach.
